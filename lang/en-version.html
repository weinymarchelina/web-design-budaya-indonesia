How diverse is Indonesia?

Indonesia is an archipelagic country where every region has its own cultural characteristics. These differences also bring about the inexhaustible cultural heritage of our country.

According to a poll by ROUGH GUIDES

Indonesia was ranked as the sixth most beautiful country in the world in 2019

In addition to stunning landscapes, Indonesia's appeal also lies in its diverse culture.


Unique customs, beautiful dances, and exquisite handicrafts, captivate the hearts of international tourists.


Thanks to this cultural diversity, Indonesia has become famous in the world and is already familiar to foreigners.

Despite the striking differences in each region, unity can still be achieved with an attitude of tolerance

The diversity of Indonesia is a gift that cannot be bought in the world. The vastly different cultures of each place should not be the reason for social discord.


Appreciating and loving differences strengthens the sense of unity throughout Indonesia.

This special diversity is a treasure we want to share with the world. We invite you to explore Indonesia's cultural treasures.

Come and be part of us

No matter where you come from, we always warmly welcome you on your way to an unforgettable cultural experience.


You can start learning and appreciating the local traditions, or even join us in introducing the diverse beauty of Indonesian culture to countries around the world.
